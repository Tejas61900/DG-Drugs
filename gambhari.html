Therapeutic Uses
  1. Raktapitta: The flower of Gambhari with honey is useful in diarrhea.
  2. Jwara: Cold infusion of Gambhari powder with sugar is useful in Paittik Jwar (fever).
  3. Sothahara: Root decoction with Pippali Churna is useful in inflammations.

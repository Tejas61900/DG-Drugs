Therapeutic Uses

  • Krimi - Vidanga powder with honey is useful for the expulsion of worms in children.
  • Vibandh - Powder with hot water and Draksha is given in constipation.
  • Kamla - Nasya treatment with Vidanga and Tila is given in jaundice.

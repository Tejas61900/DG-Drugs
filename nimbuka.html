Therapeutic Indication

  • Deepen (Increases appetite)
  • Hridya (Cardiotonic)

  • Atisara: Fresh lemon juice and jeera powder with normal water are useful in diarrhea.
  • Karnashoola: Oil prepared with fruit juice is beneficial in earache.
  • Aruchi: Juice with black salt is beneficial in anorexia.

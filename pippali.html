Therapeutic Uses

  • Swasa: Powder of Pippali, Amla, and Sunthi with honey is given in asthma and excessive cough.
  • Kasa: Vardhaman Pippali Rasayana is useful in chronic cough.
  • Jirna Jwara: Pippali triturate in mortar for 64 prahara (192 hours) is useful in chronic fever.
  • Prasuta Jwara: Pippali is given with Ghrita for detoxification in the mother of a newborn baby in fever.

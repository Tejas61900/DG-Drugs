Therapeutic Indication

  • Medohara (Anti-obesity)
  • Dadruhara (Use in skin disorders)
  • Kasa-Swasahara (Anti-cold, Anti-cough)

Therapeutic Uses

  • Vatarogas: Leaves as a vegetable preparation useful in neural and musculoskeletal systems.
  • Dadru: Seed powder with buttermilk is applied in fungal infections.
  • Sidhma: Chakramarda seeds are mixed with Kanjika and applied to skin disorders.

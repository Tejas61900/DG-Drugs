Ahiphena (Papaver somniferum)

The Powerful Opium Poppy

Introduction

Ahiphena, also known as Papaver somniferum, is a significant herb in Ayurvedic medicine. It belongs to the Papaveraceae family and is commonly referred to as the Opium Poppy.

Common Names & Synonyms

English Names: Opium Poppy, Poppy Capsule, Peon Poppy

Regional Names:

  • Bengali: Aaphi, Aphim, Pasto
  • Gujarati: Aphina, Apphou, Khuskhus
  • Hindi: Afim, Dukpunj, Khaskhas
  • Kannada: Afim, Aphen, Gasagase
  • Malayalam: Afium, Avin, Kasakasa
  • Marathi: Aphu, Aphin, Khaskhasiye
  • Punjabi: Afim, Doda, Khashkhash
  • Tamil: Abhini, Kasokasa
  • Telugu: Abhini, Gasugasala
  • Urdu: Khaskhashsufaid

Main Synonyms:

Ahiphen: Common reference in traditional texts.

Chosa: Reflects its traditional uses.

Khasakhas: Widely used name in various regions.

Morphology

Ahiphena is an erect, rarely branched annual herb, ranging from 60 to 120 cm in height. Its useful parts include seeds, seed oil, unripe capsules, flowers, and latex.

Classification

As per Bhavmishra: Classified under Haritkyadi Varga.

Phytoconstituents

Ahiphena contains several important phytoconstituents:

  • Morphine
  • Apomorphine
  • Codeine
  • Narcotine
  • Papaverene
  • Pseudo Morphine

Rasa Panchak

Rasa: Tikta, Kashaya

Guna: Laghu, Ruksha, Suksma, Vyavayi, Vikashi

Virya: Ushna

Vipaka: Katu

Prabhav: Madak

Therapeutic Uses

Ahiphena has a range of therapeutic uses:

  • Dandruff: Seeds pounded with milk and applied help in treating dandruff.
  • General Tonic: Seeds used in various recipes as a general tonic.
  • Epilepsy: Useful in treating seizures.
  • Diarrhea: Effective in controlling diarrhea.
  • Insomnia: Helps in addressing sleep disorders.

Dosage

The recommended dosages are:

  • Exudate: 30 to 125 mg
  • Khas Khas: 1 to 3 gm

Fatal Dose:

  • Opium: 2 gm
  • Morphine: 0.2 gm
  • Codeine: 0.5 gm

Adverse Effects

Overdose of Ahiphena can cause:

  • Drowsiness
  • Sleep
  • Respiratory Arrest
  • Depression
  • Potentially Fatal Effects

Remedial Measures

In case of overdose, immediate measures include:

  • Stomach wash
  • Emesis
  • Use of antidotes like ghee and tobacco
  • Cardiac stimulants like kasturi

Contraindications

Ahiphena should be avoided in:

  • Children
  • Individuals with kidney disease
  • Depression-like mental disorders

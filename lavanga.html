Action

Kapha-pittahara

Therapeutic Indication

  • Deepen (appetizer)
  • Pachan (digestive)
  • Chakshusya (good for eyes)
  • Chardighna (anti-emetic)
  • Kasa-swasahara (anti-cough and anti-asthmatic)

Therapeutic Uses

  • Kasa - Powder with honey is given in dry cough.
  • Vatarti - Powder of lavanga is applied with water to the affected area of pain.
  • Chardi - Powder with lemon juice and sugar is beneficial in vomiting.
  • Visuchika - Lavanga medicated water is given to the patient with cholera.

Dose

Powder: 1-2 gm, Oil: 1-2 drops

Chakramarda Drug Information

Basonym of Drug
Chakramarda
Main Synonym
  • Aedgaja
  • Prapunnada
  • Dadrughna
  • Meshlochana
  • Padmata
  • Chakri
  • Pinnata
Regional Name
  • Bengali: Chakunda, Panevar
  • Gujarati: Kuvadiyo
  • Hindi: Chakvad, Pavad, Panvar
  • Kannada: Tagache
  • Tamil: Ushidure
  • Telugu: Tagirish
Botanical Name
Cassia tora Linn.
Family
Caesalpiniaceae
Classification (Gana)

Acharya Charaka: Chakramarda is mentioned in Shaka Varga.

In Bhavprakash Nighantu: Mentioned in Haritakyadi Varga.

External Morphology
Annual 1 to 1.5-meter high herb
Useful Parts
Seeds, Leaves, and Root
Important Phytoconstituent
Emodin, Rubrofusarin, Fistucacidin, Questin, Obtusin, Cassiaside
Rasa Panchak
  • Rasa: Katu
  • Guna: Laghu, Ruksha
  • Virya: Ushna
  • Vipaka: Katu
Action
Kaphavatahara
Therapeutic Indication
  • Medohara (Anti-Obesity)
  • Dadruhara (Use in Skin Disorders)
  • Kasa-Swasahara (Anti-Cold, Anti-Cough)
Therapeutic Uses
  1. Vatarogas: Leaves as vegetable preparation are useful in neural and musculoskeletal systems.
  2. Dadru: Seed powder with buttermilk is applied in fungal infections.
  3. Sidhma: Chakramarda seeds mixed with Kanjika are applied to skin disorders.
Dose
Leaf Juice - 10-20 ml, Seed Powder - 1 to 3 gm
Formulations
  • Chakramarda Taila
  • Edagajadi Lepa
  • Dadrughni Vati

Basonym of Drug

Bhumi-amalaki

Main Synonym

  • Tamalaki
  • Bhudhatri
  • Bahuphala
  • Bahupatra

Regional Name

  • Bengali: Bhui Amla
  • Gujarati: Bhoyamli
  • Hindi: Bhumi Amla
  • Kannada: Kirunelli
  • Telugu: Nel Vusari

Botanical Name

Phyllanthus niruri / Phyllanthus amarus

Family

Euphorbiaceae

Classification (Gana)

  • Acharya Charak mentioned in Kasahara and Swasahara Mahakashaya
  • Acharya Vagbhata mentioned in Kasahara and Swasahara Gana

External Morphology

Small erect shrub

Useful Parts

  • The whole plant
  • Root

Important Phytoconstituent

Phyllanthin, Hypophyllanthin, Nirphyllin, Phyltetralin

Rasa Panchak

  • Rasa: Tikta, Kashaya, Madhura
  • Guna: Laghu, Ruksha
  • Virya: Shita
  • Vipaka: Madhura

Action

Kaphapittahara

Therapeutic Indication

  • Rochana (Increase appetite)
  • Kasa-Swashara (Anti-cold/cough)
  • Raktapittahara (Bleeding disorders)
  • Kamlahara (Liver disorders)

Therapeutic Uses

  • Kamla - Paste of the whole plant with buttermilk is beneficial for jaundice.
  • Prameha - Bhumi Amalaki with black pepper is beneficial in diabetes.
  • Charmaroga - Leaves paste is applied in skin disorders.

Dose

  • Powder - 3-5 gm
  • Paste - 5-10 gm

Formulations

  • Bhunimbadi Kadha

Adverse Effect

Not Known

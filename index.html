Guduchi (Giloy) Ayurvedic Herb

About Guduchi (Giloy)

Guduchi, also known as Giloy, is a famous Ayurvedic herb extensively used in the treatment of various ailments such as fever, diabetes, urinary tract disorders, anemia, jaundice, asthma, and cardiac disorders. It is highly regarded as an immuno-modulator herb that helps correct auto-immunity.

Botanical Name

Tinospora cordifolia Wall. ex Seringe

Family

Menispermaceae (Guduchi kula)

How to Consume Guduchi?

The recommended way to consume Giloy is by making its Kashayam (decoction):

  • For adults: Mix a tablespoon of Giloy powder with 2 cups of water, boil and reduce to half a cup, filter, and administer within 8 hours of preparation.

Guduchi is also available in other forms:

  • Guduchi capsule and Guduchi Ghanavati (tablets) are available in the market.

Home Remedies

  • Guduchi along with Ghee (clarified butter) is useful in Vata imbalance disorders like arthritis.
  • Guduchi with jaggery helps relieve constipation.
  • Giloy with sugar candy is helpful in Pitta imbalance disorders such as skin diseases, gastritis, and liver disorders.
  • Giloy with castor oil relieves gout.
  • Giloy with ginger powder is beneficial in rheumatoid arthritis.
  • For Pitta type of vomiting and other conditions, specific preparations are recommended.
  • For seasonal allergies, Giloy powder or its Satva (extract powder) is administered with Trikatu and turmeric paste.

Qualities and Effects on Tridosha

  • Rasa (Taste): Kashaya (Astringent), Tikta (Bitter)
  • Vipaka (Taste Conversion After Digestion): Madhura (Sweet)
  • Guna (Qualities): Laghu (Light), Snigdha (Oily), Unctuous
  • Veerya (Potency): Ushna (Hot)
  • Effect on Tridosha: Balances Vata, Pitta, and Kapha

Uses and Indications

Guduchi is beneficial in various conditions including fever, digestive disorders, skin diseases, diabetes, urinary disorders, respiratory problems, and more. It acts as a rejuvenative, improves digestion, and relieves several ailments.

Part Used

The stem of Guduchi is predominantly used in medicinal preparations.

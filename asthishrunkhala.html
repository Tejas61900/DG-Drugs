Therapeutic Indication
  • Chakshushya (Good for Eyes)

  • Bhagna Sandhanaka (Fracture Healer)
Therapeutic Uses
  1. Asthibhagna: Ashthishrunkhala powder with milk and ghee is useful in fracture healing.
  2. Arsha: Ashthishrunkhala powder with buttermilk is beneficial in piles.
  3. Pumsanvanan: For healthy progeny, use a paste of Ashthishrunkhala with its juice and oil in equal parts. Make a ball and consume after menstruation.
Dose
Fresh juice - 10-20 ml, Paste - 5-7 gm

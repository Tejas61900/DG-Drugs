Rasa Panchak

  • Vipaka: Katu

Action

Vatakaphahara

Therapeutic Indication

  • Deepen (Increase appetite)
  • Pachana (Digestive)
  • Grahi (Useful to treat diarrhea, dysentery)

Therapeutic Uses

  • Kamla - Leaves juice is beneficial in jaundice.
  • Pittatisara - Fruit pulp of Bilva is useful to treat diarrhea.
  • Grahani - Unripe fruit pulp of Bilva is beneficial in dysentery.

Dose

  • Leaf Juice - 10-20 ml
  • Powder - 3-5 gm
  • Decoction - 50-100 ml

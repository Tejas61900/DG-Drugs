Ayugram Fundraiser Program

On the occasion of Acharya Charaka Jaynati, our college team of 15 students is going to participate in the Ayugram Fundraiser Program. We will be conducting medical camps for 7 days in the village.

About Sukhayu

Sukhayu is dedicated to promoting health and wellness through the principles of Ayurveda. Our mission is to provide accessible healthcare to rural areas and educate people about the benefits of natural healing practices.
